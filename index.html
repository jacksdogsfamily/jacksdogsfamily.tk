The Jack's Dogs Family

On July 16, 2012, the Sun Brothers introduced a brand-new comic strip with unlikely known hero, the pet owner Jack. “The Jack's Dogs Family” was born. Soon, readers will come to know and love a whole cast of multiple animal characters: A clumsy little dog named Dolinj, etc. But no one stole the show quite like Espeñiol, the coolest dog on the planet. From humble beginnings on the Internet, The creators of the Jack's Dogs Family franchise wants to make the whole world laugh again with joy. Making it one day the best-loved comic strip in history.
